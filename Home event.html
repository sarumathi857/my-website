Welcome To Home Page



Welcome to the official website of our college’s annual Tech Fest — a dynamic celebration of innovation, creativity, and technology. This exciting event brings together students from all departments to showcase their talents, participate in technical competitions, and engage in collaborative learning. The Tech Fest serves as a platform to highlight students' skills in coding, web designing, app development, robotics, and more. Through a variety of contests, workshops, and exhibitions, it encourages a spirit of healthy competition and hands-on experience. The event not only promotes academic excellence but also enhances teamwork, leadership, and communication among participants. Visitors can explore a diverse range of events and take part in exciting challenges tailored to different interests and skill levels. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a beginner, or just curious, there’s something for everyone to enjoy and learn. Join us as we ignite minds, build connections, and celebrate the power of technology. Let’s make this year’s Tech Fest a memorable and inspiring journey for all!
